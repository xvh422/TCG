Sonic Voltage is a high-energy rock band hailing from the heart of the urban jungle. With a sound that fuses elements of classic rock, punk, and a hint of blues, this electrifying quartet is here to make your eardrums quake and your heart race. The band is fronted by the enigmatic lead singer and guitarist, Johnny Thunderstruck, whose lightning-fast riffs and soulful lyrics are guaranteed to leave you thunderstruck.

On bass, we have Vixen Viper, the mysterious and fiercely talented musician who slithers across the stage like a rock 'n' roll serpent, laying down the grooves that keep the crowd moving. Drummer extraordinaire, Crash Kinetic, brings a powerhouse of rhythm and thunderous beats, leaving a trail of musical destruction in his wake. Last but not least, the mad scientist on keys, Dr. Voltage, adds an otherworldly layer of synths and samples that transport you to a sonic dimension you never knew existed.

Sonic Voltage is not just a band; it's an experience. With their electrifying stage presence, they'll have you hooked from the first power chord. Get ready to be swept up in a storm of sound and fury that only Sonic Voltage can deliver. Let their sonic waves wash over you, leaving you charged with an unforgettable rock 'n' roll experience.
